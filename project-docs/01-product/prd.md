# Product Requirements Document

## Product

`creato` is a Mongolian-first AI creative studio for businesses that need product images, posters, and social content without writing prompts.

## Audience

- Small and medium businesses in Mongolia.
- Online shops and product sellers.
- Restaurants and cafes.
- Beauty, fashion, and service businesses.
- Teams that need faster visual content creation.

## Phase 1 Requirements

- Build a premium dark homepage.
- Explain the product direction clearly in Mongolian.
- Present `Category -> Product` structure.
- Show static credit pricing only in the pricing section.
- Keep the implementation frontend-only.

## Non-Requirements

- No backend.
- No auth.
- No admin.
- No payment.
- No wallet.
- No AI generation.
- No upload flow.
- No database.
